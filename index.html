About HackUnion

HackUnion is an independent builders community where students grow through real execution, not just theory. Members learn coding, product thinking, and teamwork by building and shipping together.

Affiliated with Lords Skill Academy

HackUnion is proudly affiliated with Lords Skill Academy to provide mentorship structure, quality learning pathways, and student-focused development opportunities.
